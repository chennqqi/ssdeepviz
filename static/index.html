The algorithm defined in ssdeep paper for rolling-hash:

$$ y = y - x \\ y = y + size \cdot d \\ x = x + d \\ x = x - window[c\bmod{size}] \\ window[c \bmod size] = d \\ c = c + 1 \\ z = z \ll 5 \\ z = z \oplus d $$

where the return is $x + y + z$





Note, all arrays here flow from right to left for ease of comparison with the MSB <- LSB bit array(s)
